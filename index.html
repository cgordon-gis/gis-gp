Introduction to GIS for Geophysicists

This site contains the exercises for the Introduction to GIS for geophysicists course at University of Leeds.

The worksheets are available as the GIS for Geophysicists website and as a downloadable Word document if you would like to print a copy or make your own notes on the document. Both versions will be updated as necessary during the course. Unfortunately the internal links (cross-references) will only work properly in the web version.

Details of the software required (QGIS) and how to gain access to it will be given to you before the start of the course.
